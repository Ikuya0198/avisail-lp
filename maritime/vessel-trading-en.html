Vessel Specializations

We specialize in high-demand categories for global trade, island logistics, and port operations.

Coasters

500 - 5,000 DWT

Short-sea shipping vessels ideal for coastal and inter-island trade routes (Southeast Asia, Mediterranean, Caribbean). Sourced primarily from Japan's rigorous maintenance fleets (avg. age 15-20 years, well-maintained). Suitable for bulk cargo, containers, general cargo. Typical features: single/double deck, geared/gearless options, fuel-efficient engines.

Tugboats

1,000 - 5,000 HP

Essential for port operations (harbor towing, berthing assistance), offshore support (platform supply, anchor handling), and salvage. High-quality Japanese engineering ensures reliability and fuel efficiency. Azimuth stern drive (ASD) models available. Strong bollard pull (30-80 tons typical). FiFi (fire-fighting) systems equipped on select models.

Landing Craft

LCT / LCM

Specialized for shallow-water logistics (beach landing, remote island supply) and infrastructure development. Critical for emerging markets lacking deep-water ports. Bow ramp design for direct cargo loading/unloading. Typical payload: 100-500 tons. Suitable for transporting heavy equipment, construction materials, vehicles. Japanese LCTs known for durability in harsh conditions.
