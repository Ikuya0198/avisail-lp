Our Business

Vessel Trading

Global matching service connecting Asia to the world. We combine AI-driven inventory updates with secure payment structures including ETF-based options and escrow services.

Specializing in Coaster, Tugboat, and Landing Craft vessels, we source from Japan, Korea, and Eastern Europe while serving markets across the Middle East and Asia.

Investment

AI-driven asset management through our proprietary Aviscore platform. We focus on stability and growth through disciplined execution, combining FX automation with ETF-based portfolio strategies.

Our high-yield, low-risk carry trade approach serves high-net-worth individuals seeking sophisticated asset management solutions.

Technology

Implementation-first AI development including ADCS (Active Defense Cyber Security) for SMEs and municipalities worldwide.

We integrate data infrastructure, evaluation frameworks, and safety protocols to deliver real-world operational solutions that work from day one.
