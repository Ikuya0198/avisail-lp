Features

Multi-dimensional Risk Assessment Framework

Evaluate AI infrastructure operational continuity from three perspectives

1

Credit Risk Analysis

Evaluates operator financial health, customer concentration, and contract continuity. Quantifies how operator viability impacts investment value.

2

Operational Risk Analysis

Identifies and evaluates single points of failure including power supply redundancy, cooling system reliability, and network connectivity stability.

3

Cyber Risk Analysis

Evaluates security posture, vulnerability management, and incident response capabilities. Analyzes operational disruption risk from cyber attacks.

+

Dependency Mapping

Visualizes external dependencies including power utilities, cooling system vendors, and network providers.

+

Time-series Risk Tracking

Tracks risk indicator changes over time to identify trends. Provides early warning signals for emerging risks.

+

Actionable Recommendations

Based on assessment results, proposes risk mitigation measures and additional due diligence items with clear prioritization.
