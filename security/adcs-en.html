CTEM — Continuous Threat Exposure Management

What is CTEM?

Available on Standard plan and above. Continuous threat exposure management — beyond the point-in-time scan.

Traditional security assessments are "snapshots at a single point in time." But attackers try new techniques every day, and new vulnerabilities (CVEs) are published every week.

CTEM continuously measures "how exposed are you to attackers right now" and alerts you before risks worsen. It's the next-generation security framework selected by Gartner as a Top 10 Strategic Technology.

ADCS CTEM:
Accumulates scan data and automatically updates monthly risk score trends, industry average comparisons, and attack path simulations. Instantly surface insights like "security score declining despite fixes" or "this area is becoming rapidly more dangerous."
