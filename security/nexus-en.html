Features

5-Layer Risk Assessment Framework

Multi-dimensional evaluation of strategic material supply chain risks

1

Supply Concentration

Evaluates mining/production country concentration, corporate concentration, and single-source dependency to quantify supply disruption risk.

2

Midstream Chokepoint

Analyzes geographic concentration of refining/processing capacity, technology barriers, and availability of alternative capacity.

3

Geopolitical Risk

Assesses geopolitical vulnerabilities including export controls, policy change risk, sanctions exposure, and alliance relationships.

4

Constraint Risk (ESG)

Analyzes operational constraint factors including environmental regulations, permitting requirements, social opposition, and ESG exclusion risk.

5

Substitutability (Reference)

Provides reference information on substitute technology maturity, time to commercialization, and cost gaps.

+

Actionable Recommendations

Based on assessment results, proposes specific risk mitigation measures with clear prioritization.
