FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What kind of company is Avisail Technology?
Avisail is a technology company operating in three domains: maritime business, security business, and AI development, all based on AI technology. With an implementation-first approach, we achieve both high speed and quality even with a small team.
Can both corporations and individuals use your services?
Yes. Vessel trading and cybersecurity primarily target corporations, while some system development services are also available to individuals.
Do you support global operations?
Yes. Avisail is designed for transactions across Asia, the Middle East, and Europe, enabling multilingual, multi-currency, and internationally compliant support.
How do you approach security and privacy?
Data protection and privacy are our top priorities. We implement communication encryption, access management, and regular vulnerability assessments.
Can we discuss partnerships or collaborations?
Yes, we actively welcome such discussions. Please select "Partnership" in the contact form. We welcome technical partnerships, sales agencies, and joint development.
